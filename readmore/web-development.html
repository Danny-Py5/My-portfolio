Front-End Development

Front-end development is all approximately developing what customers see and engage with once they go to a website. I use present day equipment and strategies to make your website works properly on all devices, from computer systems to cellular phones.

Here are what I can do for you:

  • Responsive Design: Making your website correct and works easily on any device, whether or not it`s a small telecellsmartphone or a large laptop screen.
  • Interactive Features: Adding animations, dropdowns, sliders, and different functions that make your website energetic and engaging.
  • Optimization: Ensuring everyone, including people with disabilities, can use your website easily
  • Web Accessibility: Ensuring everyone, consisting of human beings with disabilities, can use your website easily.

Some of the equipment I use:

  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript: The constructing blocks of websites.
  • Framework like React: For developing dynamic and effective applications. When you work with me, I ensure that your website not only looks great but is also easy to use for your visitors.
